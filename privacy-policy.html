Privacy Policy

Effective Date: TBD  •  Last Updated: TBD
Contact: Cavidez@gmail.com
We do not collect your wallet private keys, seed phrases, or passwords. Never share them with anyone. If you use blockchain features, some data is inherently public on-chain. See Risk & Disclaimer.

1. What We Collect

a) Discord-related data

  • Discord user ID, username, and avatar (as provided by Discord)
  • Server/channel context needed to operate commands (where applicable)
  • Gameplay data (characters, inventory, currency, stats, progression)

b) Blockchain-related data (optional)

  • Public wallet address(es) you choose to connect or use
  • On-chain transaction references (public by nature)

c) Chance/Gambling feature data (optional)

  • Records of wagers/entries, outcomes, and cooldown/limit checks to prevent abuse

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Wallet private keys or seed phrases
  • Payment card info (unless you use a third-party processor outside our control)
  • Government ID or sensitive personal identifiers (unless required by law, which we currently do not request)

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain gameplay features
  • Store progress, balances, and inventory
  • Detect fraud, abuse, cheating, and exploit behavior
  • Provide support and troubleshoot issues
  • Improve balancing, performance, and user experience

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share or disclose information only:

  • If required by law or valid legal process
  • To investigate or prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • With service providers necessary to run the Services (hosting, databases, analytics, etc.)

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the Services, resolve disputes, enforce rules, and comply with legal obligations. We may delete or anonymize data after long inactivity or account termination.

6. Your Choices & Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, or deletion of data associated with your account. Email Cavidez@gmail.com with your Discord user ID for verification.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data, but no system is perfectly secure. You are responsible for securing your Discord account and (if applicable) your wallet and device.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy by posting a revised version. Continued use after the effective date of changes indicates acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Cavidez@gmail.com.
